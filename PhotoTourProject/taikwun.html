Tai Kwun is a vibrant cultural and heritage center located in the heart of Hong Kong’s Central district. Once the city’s Central Police Station compound, it has been beautifully restored and transformed into a space that blends history with contemporary art, performances, and dining. Visitors can explore its historic architecture, attend exhibitions or live shows, and learn about Hong Kong’s colonial-era law enforcement in the on-site museum. Tai Kwun is a perfect mix of old and new, offering a unique and enriching experience for locals and tourists alike.
