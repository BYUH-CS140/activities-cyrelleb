The Ten Thousand Buddhas Monastery in Sha Tin is one of Hong Kong’s most unique and peaceful spiritual sites. Nestled on a hillside, the journey to the top includes a scenic climb past hundreds of golden Buddha statues, each with its own distinct pose and expression. The main temple houses nearly 13,000 miniature Buddha figures lining the walls, creating an awe-inspiring atmosphere. Despite its name, the monastery is not home to monks, but rather a cultural and historical landmark maintained by volunteers. It’s a must-visit for those looking to experience tranquility, culture, and breathtaking views all in one place.
